Baby development Journey

Discover the incredible milestones of your
child's first three years

0-3 Months

  • Moves limbs
  • Tracks objects
  • Smiles responsively
  • Makes cooing sound

3-6 Months

  • Lifts head when on stomach
  • Grasps objects with both hands
  • Laughs, likes mirrors
  • Sits with support, rolls by 6 mo

6-9 Months

  • Sits unaided, crawls
  • Finds hidden objects
  • Picks objects with thumb & finger
  • Waves goodbye, separation anxiety

9-12 Months

  • Stands unaided, explores objects
  • Plays peek-a-boo, imitates actions
  • Points body parts, responds to name
  • Uses gestures, says 2â€“3 words

12-18 Months

  • Walks by 15 mo, runs by 18 mo
  • Follows commands, names objects/pictures
  • Scribbles, solves simple puzzles
  • Helps dress, attempts feeding

18-24 Months

  • Climbs stairs, jumps by 24 mo
  • Stacks 5 to 7 cubes
  • Uses 3-word sentences, pretend play
  • Handles spoon, helps undress

24-36 Months

  • Rides tricycle, throws ball
  • Counts objects, enjoys stories
  • Plays games, shows empathy
  • Helps dress, follows basic rules

About Us
